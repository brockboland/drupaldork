About

Hi! I'm Brock Boland, and I like Drupal a little more than is socially acceptable.

I live and work in Washington, DC, and have been developing for Drupal since December 2009. This site was launched in late 2010 to give me a place to write about Drupal specifically and web development generally, since (I assume) many of the readers of my personal blog had no interest in either.

Please be assured that any opinions I express here (especially the controversial ones) are mine and mine alone, and do not necessarily match those of my employer (I say that because my supervisor has strong feelings about Panels and may fire me for a misstep).
